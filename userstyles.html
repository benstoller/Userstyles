IMLeagues adblock

Description:
IMLeagues is one of the worst, most intrusive ad filled website I have ever used. It is required to sign up for any intramural sport at jmu. So this userstyles sets all the adds to not be displayed. I wish there was a way to just remove them so they didn't slow down my computer like they do, but this is definitely a huge improvement. Also am I allowed to legally publish/share this code? I'm concerned with this one because it is taking ad money away from the website.

Code:
.ds_cpp, .videoRight, video, .iml-head-ads, .iframe-ads-holder, .iml-square-banner, .iframe-ads-holder, .d-inline-block, #ad_unit, .ad-standalone-img, .Advertise, [id^=ad], [class^=ad], .iml-banner.minute-media-player, .teads-inread, #imlFootBanner {

}
